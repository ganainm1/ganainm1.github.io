The hospital and shop are closed between 1PM and 2PM daily.

Our shop attached to the hospital offers a large array of pet food, including Hills and Burns. We also stock a selection of pet accessories including Leads and collars, pet treats and toys and grooming products.

This is all alongside our flea, tick and worming programmes, which offers free advice from our nursing staff on hand. And a wide array of large animal remedies, anti-parasitics, hygiene products. Again with professional advice on hand at no extra cost.
