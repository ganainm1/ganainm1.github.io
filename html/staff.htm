Veterinary Surgeons

William Burgess M.V.B. M.R.C.V.S.

Position: Partner

History: Born Co. Carlow qualified from U.C.D 1978. Worked in Northern Ireland, England and Wexford in mixed veterinary practice. Moved to Drogheda in 1987. Founder member of Westgate Veterinary Hospital.

Special interest: Large Animal Medicine.

Simon Rath M.V.B. M.R.C.V.S.

Position: Partner

History: From Co. Wexford qualified from U.C.D 1985. Worked in England and Wexford (Small animal practice) and Saudi Arabia (Dairy Health). He moved to Drogheda in 1996.

Special interest: Ultrasonography and Dairy Health/ reproduction.

John Russell M.V.B. M.R.C.V.S.

Position: Partner

History: From Tullyallen Drogheda qualified from U.C.D 1984. Worked in U.C.D (house surgeon), Cork, Castlebar (mixed veterinary practice) and England (small animal specialist clinic).

Special interest: Small animal medicine/surgery and dermatology.

Jasone Elorza Spillane LDA Vet

History: Born in Bilbao, Spain. Qualified from Zaragoza, Spain 1990. Worked in several small animal practices including PDSA in Middlesboro, Leeds, Manchester and Dublin. She also worked in UCD teaching vet students Anaesthesiology. Joined Westgate Veterinary Hospital in 1998.

Special interest: Feline medicine and exotic pets.

Paul Lawlor M.V.B.
