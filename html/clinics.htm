Westgate Hospital

Westgate Veterinary Hospital, which is located in Scarlet Street, Drogheda, was opened in 1986. At the hospital we run three pet clinics per day Monday through Friday, with two further clinics on a Saturday. These clinics can be extremely busy therefore we do recommend making an appointment to reduce your waiting time, however walk ins will also be seen during these clinics.

We also provide a 24 hour on call emergency service with a veterinary surgeon and veterinary nurse available.

Our hospital facilities include an in-house laboratory, x-ray, ultrasound, dentals and orthopedic surgeries, nurses consultations, as well as routine surgeries and consultations. We also have facilities for hospitalisation and intensive care if your pet should require it.

Here at Westgate Veterinary Hospital we have a true understanding of how much your pets mean to you and as such we strive to provide the highest standard of care possible.

Our nurses are always available for friendly advice either face to face or over the phone. If you have any queries call us, drop in or check out our contact page which has a question submission form which will bring your query to us via e-mail and we respond to you by e-mail.

Laytown Clinic

Laytown veterinary clinic was opened in 2002 as an addition to our main practice in Drogheda, Co. Louth. It has proven to be an extremely convenient for our clients particularly at peak traffic times.

We stock a limited amount of Burns and Hills food, however food and medication can be ordered through Westgate hospital to be collected in the Laytown branch.

Currently we are operating as a consultation only practice but our expansion to a full time practice is underway so watch this space!
