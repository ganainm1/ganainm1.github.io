Services

Our available services include routine surgery (neutering, spaying, etc.), soft tissue surgery, dentistry, ultrasound, digital xray, orthopedic surgery, in house laboratory, and an isolation unit for infectious diseases.

Pet Check Clinics

We are now running free pet check clinics. These nurse appointments are to assist owners with the basics of pet care. These appointments are FREE OF CHARGE and are for new pets introduced to the household, not just new pups and kittens.

These nurse clinics are appointment only, please ring the hospital for an appointment.

Other FREE nurse clinics are run for stitch removal, wound checks and dental checks.

Weight Clinic

Our nurses run FREE weight management clinics. Helen (R.V.N.) is our specialist nurse.

Pets that are overweight have an initial consultation and a check up every 2 weeks until the target weight has been achieved.

The weight clinics have become a huge success and with obesity in pets becoming a national problem we hope their popularity will continue.

Obesity in an animal can contribute to many medical problems, so if you think your pet may be overweight please contact us for a FREE weight check today!

Free weight advice is also available at www.hillspetslimmer.co.uk.
