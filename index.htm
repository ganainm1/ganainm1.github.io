Welcome

Book an appointment on 041-9837658 or 041-9838718

Westgate Veterinary Hospital is a Veterinary Council accredited practice situated in Scarlet Street, Drogheda. We have 6 Veterinary Surgeons, 7 Veterinary Nurses and multiple admin staff, and have been providing veterinary services throughout the Louth and Meath area since 1986

With constantly advancing diagnostics, medicines and an extensive fully qualified veterinary staff we aim to provide your pets with the highest standard of veterinary care within a warm and welcoming environment.

We run daily clinics. Appointments are available and recommended to minimise waiting times for our clients.

Mon - Fri:

  • 9.00am – 10.30am
  • 2.00pm – 3.30pm
  • 5.00pm – 6.30pm

Saturday:

  • 9.00am – 11.00am
  • 2.00pm – 3.30pm

We have ample free off road parking and a large range of up to date in house diagnostics to ensure the most convenient visit for you and your pet.

We also provide our own 24 hour out of hours emergency service, ensuring it is one of our Vets that answer your call.
